Transhumanist Dilemma

Transhumanism, is an ideology centering around technology in extending the human life. Both in the sense of life span, with promises living to hundreds of years old, and also in the sense of what it means to be human. As the boundary begins to break down between man and machine, what it means to be human will inevitably change.
